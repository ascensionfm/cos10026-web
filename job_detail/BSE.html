#00009

Salary
80M - 100M
Level
Senior
Type
Full Time
Location

Bridge Engineer: Job Descriptions
  • A Bridge System Engineer will play a role as a communication channel between the developing team in Vietnam and clients in Japan.
  • Directly communicate with clients via email, skype (or chartwork tool) to meet requirements.
  • Manage project timeline, report to clients on regular basis, and manage project performance.
  • Project forms: all web-application systems (using Ruby language, PHP, Java, ASP, NET), all game applications on smartphones (iOS, Android).
Bridge Engineer: Job Qualification
Ruby ASP.NET Java PHP
  • Japanese skill at least N2
  • At least 3 years experience in a similar position.
  • Prefer candidate with work experience or study in Japan.
  • Good understanding of object-oriented programming.
  • Have experience using at least one of these languages: ASP.NET, PHP, Java, Ruby, Cobol.
  • Leadership potentiality, high responsibility, good teamwork spirit.
  • Experience managing big projects.
  • Understanding Japanese culture and Japanese working style.
Bridge Engineer: Benefits
  • Salary range: Up to 100M/year
  • House rent subsidy
  • Review: 1 time/year in October.
  • Young and dynamic working environment.
  • Have the opportunity to go onsite in Singapore, and Australia…
  • Opportunity to approach the newest technology trends
  • Sponsor and encourage staff to study courses by covering tuition fees, such as the Udemy course.
  • Extra Medical Insurance and Annual health check-ups.
  • A dynamic, comfortable, professional working environment, with a gaming machine, and music playing hours as required between breaks and after working hours.
  • Other benefits: Summer vacation, birthday, team building… are held periodically.
