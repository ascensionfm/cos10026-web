We are looking for AI Engineer to join our dynamic team!

Responsibilities
  • Design, develop, and maintain AI/ML-powered applications using Python, ensuring high performance, scalability, and reliability.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new AI features and applications
  • Lead the design and implementation of robust, secure, and scalable back-end solutions for AI models.
  • Integrate machine learning algorithms into production systems, including model training, deployment, and monitoring.
  • Develop and optimize codebases for performance, ensuring low latency and high availability.
  • Collaborate with data scientists to build and deploy data pipelines, preprocess data, and manage datasets efficiently.
    • Work with cloud infrastructure (Azure/AWS/GCP) to scale machine learning models, manage resources, and ensure system reliability.
  • Continuously explore and evaluate new AI/ML frameworks, libraries, and techniques to integrate them into our AI solutions.
  • Ensure compliance with data security and privacy standards, particularly when working with sensitive or regulated data.
