Job overview

Next_Gen Corp is a global software development company in Hanoi, Vietnam with branches in Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang and Fukuoka, Japan. People at Next_Gen Corp always work hard to play harder. Our spirit is to maintain and express Youth, Creativeness and Professional in our way. Furthermore, we have created a space for you to showcase your competences.

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Production Support to join our team. The ideal candidate is a proactive problem solver, ticket management with strong analytical and communication skills. As a Production Support, you will play a vital role in helping our team to receive IT-related concerns, and then proceed to diagnose and solve these issues. You should also ensure the regular maintenance.

Responsibilities
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to gather and document business requirements.
  • Analyze data and processes to identify areas for improvement and efficiency.
  • Provide first/second level contact and problem resolution for customer issues
  • Work with Vendors, Stakeholder to remediate complex issues as needed.
  • Conduct research and market analysis to support decision-making.
  • Conduct UAT and training.
  • Prepare and present reports and findings to management.
  • Participate in meetings, workshops, and brainstorming sessions to drive innovation.
  • Provide timely communication on issue status and resolution.
  • Maintain ticket updates for all reported incidents.
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent in Computer Science or related field.
  • At least 2+ years of experience.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • Basic understanding of business analysis concepts and methodologies.
  • Detail-oriented with a focus on accuracy and quality.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team and communicate effectively with stakeholders.
  • Proven analytical, troubleshooting and problem- solving skills.
  • Proven ability to multi-task, effectively determine priorities and meet SLA’s.
  • Excellent communication relationship-building and internal customer service skills.
  • Adaptable and flexible in a fast-changing industry and work environment.
Benefits
  • Salary: Up to 45.000.000 VND (Gross)
  • 13 months’ salary per year.
  • Join a global team and work directly with many talents around the world.
  • Evaluate salary annually.
  • Premium Health Insurance Next_Gen Care.
  • Sponsor and encourage staff to study courses by covering tuition fees, such as Udemy, Coursera.
  • Other benefits: Summer vacation, birthday, team building… are held periodically.
