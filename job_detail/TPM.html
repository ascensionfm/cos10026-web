We are looking for Technical Project Manager with knowledge of usability and understanding the specifics of Software Products, who contribute to the development phase of software products life cycle.

Responsibilities
  • Executing tests on software usability

Requirements
  • Project manager with at least 5 years of experience in software development, specific 2 years in project management.
  • Experience in working with project management tools such as Jira, Trello, MS Projects…
  • Understanding of all phases of a software development cycle.

  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum.
  • Good communication with English speaking & listening skills.
